Yile Wang is currently a postdoc at Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Yang Liu. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University (2018~2022), advised by Prof. Yue Zhang. Before that, he received his B.S./M.S. degree from Zhejiang University and also worked at Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. His primarily research interests include artificial intelligence, natural language processing and large language models.

I will join the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Shenzhen University as an Assistant Professor in August 2024. Students interested in AI, NLP, and LLMs are welcome to contact me!
